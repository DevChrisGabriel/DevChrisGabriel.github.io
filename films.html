About Us

Who We Are

We are passionate students from STI College Fairview, specializing in Computer Science. Our shared dedication to knowledge and innovation drives us to explore and contribute to various global issues.

Our Mission

At the core of our mission is the commitment to transform learning into an engaging and enjoyable experience. We focus on visual storytelling and present compact, precise information on crucial topics such as Global Civil Society, Human Rights Campaigns, Women's Rights, Transnational Solidarity, Aid and Development Initiatives, Green Activism, and World Citizenship.

What We Do

Exciting Learning: We strive to make learning an exciting journey, blending creativity with educational content to captivate our audience.

Precision Matters: Our approach involves distilling lengthy information into concise, easy-to-understand formats, ensuring that knowledge is accessible to everyone.

Contact Us

Have questions or want to delve deeper into our initiatives? Connect with us through our Facebook page for more information. We welcome your curiosity and enthusiasm for making a positive impact on a global scale.

Global Cities

Cities have been for thousands of years the centers of civilization as they have watched empires, kingdoms, governments, and corporations come and go. But in the space of just a few decades, our urban fabric is undergoing a radical transformation; today’s wave of mass urbanization is historically unprecedented in speed and scale.

Human Rights Everyone's Rights

The Sectoral Groups and ZABIDA recognize the efforts of Government Agencies in continually Promoting and Protecting Human Rights. However, we call on all Duty Bearers to continue collaborating in addressing the continuous violation of Human Rights and making the concept of Human Rights more meaningful and relevant to the marginalized sectors in the grassroots.

Louder Together: A Global Citizen Documentary

International Advocacy Organization Global Citizen and the Hyde Park Entertainment Group, the latter formed by award-winning Hollywood producer Ashok Amritraj, present Louder Together; a documentary special focusing on the groundbreaking advocacy work done by Global Citizen to end extreme poverty. The Global Citizen Festival is a moment for music fans and activists to hold world leaders accountable for their commitment towards ending extreme poverty.

Africa Rising

Discover the vibrant spirit and diverse tapestry of Africa in "Africa Rising." This compelling documentary takes you on a journey across the continent, unveiling stories of resilience, innovation, and cultural richness. From bustling urban landscapes to serene natural wonders, witness the transformative narrative of Africa as it rises, embracing its potential and shaping a dynamic future.

Consumerism

Dive into the intricate world of consumerism, where the quest for goods and services shapes not just individual choices but entire global landscapes. From ethical dilemmas to environmental concerns, this narrative explores the impact of our purchasing power on societies and economies worldwide.
